The Journey · 5 Days
01
The Departure
Delhi → Kasol
Evening departure from Delhi. Your first strangers are the ones you meet on the bus.
Overnight journey through the mountains. Ice-breakers. Music. First conversations.
The trip starts before you arrive.
02
The Arrival
Kasol
Morning arrival. Parvati River in front of you. Check-in. Breathe.
Kasol market. The famous cafés. Brownies. Local food. No plans.
Evening by the river. Watch the light change. First night in the valley.
03
Into Tosh
Kasol → Tosh
Move to Tosh. Higher altitude. Closer to the sky. Everything intensifies.
Village walks. Local market. Snow peaks on every side.
DJ night. Bonfire. The night people talk about for years.
04
Manikaran
Tosh → Manikaran
Visit Manikaran Sahib. Gurudwara. Natural hot springs. Ancient energy.
The kind of place that makes you quiet in a good way.
Evening departure for Delhi. Last mountain air.
05
The Return
Delhi
Morning arrival in Delhi. You left with strangers.
You came back with something else entirely.
Some things from Kasol stay in Kasol. Some don't.
