Common Questions
What happens if I don't get selected? +
We curate the group to ensure the best possible dynamic. If your application isn't selected for this specific batch, your ₹1,000 slot booking fee will be fully refunded within 48 hours. However, if you drop out after being selected, the fee is non-refundable.
Is this safe for solo female travelers? +
Absolutely. Our curation process involves strict screening, including social media verification and sometimes brief calls. We maintain an equal 50/50 gender ratio, and a dedicated, experienced trip lead travels with the group at all times.
Where do we depart from? +
The journey begins and ends in Delhi. We will share the exact pickup point and timing (usually around 7 PM) with the final selected candidates a few days before departure.
What kind of accommodation is provided? +
We believe in a mix of comfort and experience. You'll stay in premium Swiss camps near the river in Kasol, and comfortable boutique hotels/homestays in Tosh. Rooms are shared on a twin/triple sharing basis (gender-segregated).
